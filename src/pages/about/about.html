Our History

K-Leisure is a premier health and leisure destinations in Co. Kildare. We pride ourselves on providing an excellent and memorable service to all our customers. K-Leisure has a fully accessible pool and gym area which allows us to provide a health and leisure experience for the entire community.


Our facility has won the coveted Ireland Active White Flag award since we opened in 2009 and have retained the award ever since.


2016 was an exciting year for K Leisure Naas with the opening of a brand new gym floor and fitness studio. The new gym floor has state of the art equipment including a SKILLMILL, STAIRMASTER and WATT bikes. The new Functional Training Zone features battle ropes, boxing bags, plyometric box, TRX station, power bags and if you want to spice up your training routine, you can access our FUNXTION EXPERIENCE STATION for exercises, workouts, challenges and much more.
